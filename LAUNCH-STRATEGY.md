## Social Proof Strategy

### Testimonial Collection System

**Who to Ask:**
1. Early adopters who benefit
2. Consulting clients (after delivery)
3. Course students (after completion)
4. Sponsors (after value received)
5. Oracle colleagues (with permission)

**How to Ask:**

**Email Template:**
```
Subject: Would love your feedback on [project]

Hi [Name],

I noticed you've been using [specific thing]. How's it working for you?

If it's been helpful, would you mind sharing a quick testimonial? Just 2-3 sentences about:
- What problem it solved
- What results you got
- What you'd tell others

I'll feature it on the website with your permission.

Thanks!
Frank
```

**What to Collect:**
- Written testimonial (2-3 sentences)
- Name and title
- Company (if relevant)
- Photo (optional)
- Video (stretch goal)

**Where to Use:**
- Website landing page
- Course sales pages
- Consulting page
- Social media
- Email marketing

## Risk Mitigation

**What if no one signs up as sponsor?**
- Double down on value delivery
- Create more free content
- Offer time-limited bonuses
- Personal outreach to power users

**What if courses don't fill?**
- Delay launch, build more waitlist
- Reduce price for first cohort
- Add more bonuses
- Create urgency with limited spots

**What if consulting doesn't convert?**
- Improve case studies
- Offer lower-price entry (strategy session)
- Better qualify leads
- Improve sales process

**What if content doesn't perform?**
- Analyze what works, do more of that
- Test different formats
- Improve thumbnails/titles
- Study successful creators
